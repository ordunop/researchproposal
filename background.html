In a study done by SÃ¡nchez-Connally, they look at the effects of race, gender, and class that Latinx students experience during college. Rather than looking at a variety of racial and ethnic backgrounds that make up first-generation college students, this study looks at Latinx college students. The qualitative study uses the Critical Race Theory to describe the experiences that Latinx students experience at primarily white institutions (PWIs). The study consists of Latinx students that have already graduated from a four-year college, and they discuss the challenges they faced, and how they were able to overcome them to ultimately succeed and graduate. This study is a great example of a qualitative approach that could be applied to the research proposal at hand. The study looked at the unique challenges that Latinx students encountered in college and could be applied to the unique set of challenges that Latinx first-generation students face when seeking post-graduate employment.
